MODULE 1: SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)
========================================================

--------------------------------------------------------
SIP – BASIC CONCEPTS
--------------------------------------------------------

1. Monthly Investment
   - Har mahine invest kiya jane wala fixed amount
   - Example: ₹1,000 / ₹5,000 / ₹10,000

2. Time Period
   - Investment duration (years me)
   - Example: 5 years, 10 years, 20 years

3. Expected Return (% per year)
   - Annual estimated return
   - Commonly: 10% – 15%

4. Compounding
   - Interest par interest
   - Time badhne par returns fast grow karte hain

5. Total Invested Amount
   - Monthly Amount × Total Months

6. Maturity Amount
   - Time ke end me total value (investment + profit)

7. Profit / Gain
   - Maturity Amount – Total Invested Amount

8. Risk Level
   - Market dependent
   - Long term me risk kam hota hai

9. Investment Type
   - Equity SIP
   - Debt SIP
   - Hybrid SIP


--------------------------------------------------------
SIP – WEBSITE FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------

Frontend (React):
- Monthly Amount Input
- Investment Duration Selector (Years)
- Expected Return Input
- Calculate Button
- Result Cards:
  - Total Invested
  - Total Return
  - Net Profit
- Growth Graph (Line / Bar Chart)

Backend (Node + Express):
- SIP calculation logic
- REST API for calculation
- Validation (negative values, empty fields)

Database (MongoDB):
- User SIP history
- Saved SIP calculations
- Timestamp (date & time)

Extra Features (Optional):
- Compare multiple SIPs
- Download result as PDF
- User login based SIP history


========================================================
MODULE 2: STOCKS (Share Market)
========================================================

--------------------------------------------------------
STOCKS – BASIC CONCEPTS
--------------------------------------------------------

1. Share
   - Company ka ownership ka ek part

2. Share Price
   - Ek share ki current value

3. Market Price
   - Real-time buying/selling price

4. Quantity
   - Total shares purchased

5. Buy Price
   - Jis price par share kharida

6. Sell Price
   - Jis price par share becha

7. Profit / Loss
   - (Sell Price – Buy Price) × Quantity

8. Market Capitalization
   - Company ki total market value
   - Share Price × Total Shares

9. 52 Week High
   - Last 1 year ka highest price

10. 52 Week Low
    - Last 1 year ka lowest price

11. Volatility
    - Price me ups & downs

12. Risk Level
    - Stock wise risk different hota hai


--------------------------------------------------------
STOCKS – WEBSITE FEATURES

Extra Features (Optional):

========================================================
MODULE 3: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
========================================================

--------------------------------------------------------
IPO – BASIC CONCEPTS
--------------------------------------------------------

1. IPO
   - Company first time public ko shares bechti hai

2. Issue Price
   - IPO ke share ka price range

3. Open Date
   - IPO apply karne ka start date

4. Close Date
   - IPO apply karne ka end date

5. Lot Size
   - Minimum shares per application

6. Minimum Investment
   - Lot Size × Issue Price

7. Allotment Status
   - Shares mile ya nahi

8. Listing Date
   - Stock exchange par listing date

9. Listing Price
   - First day ka market price

10. GMP (Grey Market Premium)
    - Unofficial market premium

11. Risk Factor
    - IPO fully guaranteed nahi hota


--------------------------------------------------------
